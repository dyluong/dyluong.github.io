Additional Resources


  • PC Part Picker

    PC Part Picker is a very useful site for building a computer. This site allows you to choose PC parts, and also automatically make sure that all of you computer parts are compatible with one another. In addition, the site shows the current market value of each computer part, as well as show you how much wattage your computer will need. The wattage is estimated but still accurate and helpful for choosing power supply.
