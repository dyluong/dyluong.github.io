About me


Hi, I'm Dylan Luong! I'm currently a 3rd year computer science major.
I love computers, both their usage and the building process! I enjoy gaming,
which is how I got into building them. The building process provides both a rewarding
and fun experience.

To be honest, I've only made about two computers and repaired about three
laptops, so I'm probably not a credible person to be creating a build guide
for computers, but I figured it wouldn't hurt to share what I've learned!
